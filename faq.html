FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 01

    What is Vishwapreneur?

    Vishwapreneur is the annual national level entrepreneurial summit organized by Entrepreneurship Development Cell, VIIT. The purpose of Vishwapreneur is spread the spirit of entrepreneurship and inspire students to take the entrepreneurial route. This year however, vishwapreneur will be the one stop platform for complete understanding of how to establish a startup.

  • 02

    Who can come for Vishwapreneur?

    All students, young entrepreneurs and generally everyone who is interested in entrepreneurship is welcome.

  • 03

    Where will Vishwapreneur be hosted?

    Vishwapreneur takes place in the Sharad Arena of Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, Upper Indiranagar, Bibwewadi, Pune (pincode- 37)

  • 04

    What is the format this year?

    This year, there will be six sessions conducted by veteran entrepreneurs who are pioneers in their respective domains which include but are not limited to Idea Feasibility, Finance, Business strategies and development, Funding, Marketing and Advertising.
