Why Vishwapreneur?

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology is set to host the 3rd edition of its flagship national convention, Vishwapreneur ’15. Vishwapreneur ’15 is scheduled to be held on 2nd January 2015. It will be hosted at Sharad Arena, VIT college, Pune.

Vishwapreneur ’15 will commence with the B-Plan competition, where participants pitch their business ideas on a platform which could not only fetch them rewards amounting to 75000 INR but also the watchful eyes of angel investors.

The following 2 days will witness a national entrepreneurial convention which will give the participants an opportunity to interact on a personal level with a number of industrial tycoons.

EDC VIIT has always endeavoured to bridge the gap between the plains of entrepreneurship and engineering, and thereby create individuals who not only have the scientic know-how required to contribute to the world, but also the expertise required to produce and market the implementation of that knowledge to the entire world. Interactions with successful, reputed entrepreneurs are valuable experiences, experiences that we strive to maximise. With this objective in mind, the Entrepreneurship Development Cell proudly created VISHWAPRENEUR, a convention that provides the ideal platform for such interaction and growth. A summit where engineering students from all over, and occasionally out of Maharashtra, convene to glean what they can from our respected speakers, many of which have an engineering background themselves.

VISHWAPRENEUR can hence be regarded as not only an academy for entrepreneurial experience and sharing of ideas for the young and eager prospective entrepreneurs, but also as the pulpit from which today’s big-shot entrepreneurs pass on their knowledge to the next generation.
