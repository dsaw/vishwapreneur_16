ABOUT VISHWAPRENEUR

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology is set to host the 4th edition of its flagship national convention, Vishwapreneur ’16.

Those 2 days will witness a national entrepreneurial convention which will give the participants an opportunity to interact on a personal level with a number of industrial tycoons.

EDC VIIT has always endeavoured to bridge the gap between entrepreneurship and engineering, and thereby create individuals who not only have the scientific know-how to contribute to the world, but also the expertise to produce and market it to the entire world.
Read More

Interactions with successful, reputed entrepreneurs are valuable experiences, experiences that we strive to maximise. With this objective in mind, the Entrepreneurship Development Cell proudly created VISHWAPRENEUR, a convention that provides the ideal platform for such interaction and growth. A summit where engineering students from all over, and occasionally out of Maharashtra, convene to glean what they can from our respected speakers.

VISHWAPRENEUR can hence be regarded as not only an academy for entrepreneurial experience and sharing of ideas for the young and eager prospective entrepreneurs, but also as the pulpit from which today’s big-shot entrepreneurs pass on their knowledge to the next generation.
