Anup Tapadia

Founder, TouchMagix Media .

Anup Tapadia is the founder of TouchMagix Company. He became the youngest Origamist at age seven and acquired international recognition in Japan and Europe. At 14, the prodigy became the world’s youngest Microsoft-certified professional. At 16, he started his first company TechnoKarma Labs, where he developed a mesh router. At 19, he received his first Masters degree, after which he went to work with US-based telecommunications giant Qualcomm.

D.S.Kulkarni

Chairman, DSK Group of Companies.

He is the Chairman of DSK Group of Companies. DSK Motors; a 1100 crore turnover company, is synonymous with Toyota and vice versa. DSK Toyota is Toyota’s largest dealer in the western Asia. DSK Supinfocom is India’s 1st International Institute for Animation, Video Gaming & Industrial Design.

Atul Lahoti

President, Maheshwari Vidya Prachark Mandal.

Mr. Atul Lahoti is in the business of manufacturing of special purpose epoxide resins required by powder coating industry. He is largest distributor of powder coatings in the country. He has presented number of technical papers on powder coating technology in India and abroad. He is instrumental in setting up large powder coating plants in Maharashtra.

Umesh Dashrathi

MD, Rucha Engineers.

Mr.Dashrathi has got thorough knowledge about setting up of new plant, new production process, selection of plant & machinery and developing new products and components. He has experienced into automotive industry, auto component industry, home furnishing industry, service sector, for more 25 years. He has received many awards and accolades for his indelible mark in core industry.

Vishwas Updikar

Founder Director, Wavelet Technologies.

Wavelet Group was set up in 1995 by Vishwas Updikar, a Ph.D. in Digital Signal Processing, and the motivation to set it up was to bridge the large gap between the expectations of largely multi-disciplinary applications of DSP and the developers of DSP.

Geeta Bhansali

Founder, Live Life - Holistic Healing Centre.

She has been into Healing field for more than 12 years and actively practicing various healing modalities and therapies from her centre-‘LIVE LIFE – Holistic Healing Centre’ at Central Mumbai near ITC Grand Central Hotel. Geeta believes that everybody has an innate ability to heal himself and hence she promotes optimal wellness by providing holistic health care through an integrated approach.
