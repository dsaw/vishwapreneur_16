INTERNSHIP FAIR

About the Fair

Reducing the gap between students and the corporate world has been one of EDC's prime motives since its inception. In the newest edition of our flagship event: Vishwapreneur '16, we plan to achieve this motive by providing the students of Pune university with a fair chance to secure an internship and work with some of the most exciting companies in Pune.

The internship fair will see 30 reputed companies from across Pune, coming together at one place, and giving an opportunity to the students to work with them and receive exposure to the processes of the corporate world.

The early bird discounted registration fee is Rs 100 . You need to fill in your details online and send your resume. This data will be used to filter the companies on the basis of area of interest and skills.
The participants will come to VIIT on 8th January 2016 where those companies will have their stalls set up. The final criteria of selection will be decided by the company. And that is where you will get the golden opportunity to work with them and gain some valuable experience.

To get yourself an internship, register with us at your nearest desk. Or contact us on 7276852228
